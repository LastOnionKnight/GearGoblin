GearGoblin v0.4.0 — CharacterPanelRefined Attribution
======================================================

Portions of GearGoblin's native AtkNode injection (introduced in v0.4.0) are
derived from CharacterPanelRefined (CPR), an open-source FFXIV Dalamud plugin
that augments the in-game Character Status window with additional derived
statistics.

Specifically, the following are adapted from CPR:

  - The CloneNode<T> primitive in Util/NodeUtil.cs (verbatim port).
  - The AddStatRow injection helper in Services/StatusPanelInjector.cs
    (simplified port — original supports an hideOriginal flag and color-copy
    mode that GearGoblin does not use).
  - The hardcoded CharacterStatus addon section IDs (26 = Attributes, 36 =
    Offensive Properties, 44 = Defensive Properties, 51 = Physical Properties,
    58 = Mental Properties, 80 = Gear, 86 = Role Properties). These were
    discovered through in-game inspection by the CPR authors and reused with
    attribution.
  - The sibling-walk navigation patterns for finding Crit / Det / DH parents
    within Offensive Properties.
